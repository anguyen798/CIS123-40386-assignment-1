Doggie Definitions

Chihuahua
A small barking demon. Also very cute.
Pomeranian
A fluffy ball of hair that may be even cuter than a Chihuahua. Also pint size.
Corgi
A medium sized dog that is shaped like a hot dog
Husky
A large furry dog that is used be Canadians as their primary form of transportation
Shiba Inu
A medium sized doggo. Also known for the cryptocurrency dodgecoin meme.
